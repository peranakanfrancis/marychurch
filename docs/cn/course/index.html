The Sacraments are God’s action which He shows us the love that He gives for his sons and daughters. All them are established by God, and that is why He is the person who celebrates the Sacraments through different ways. We believe in the Seven Sacraments as outward, visible symbols of the inward, spiritual Grace, Presence and working of our Lord Jesus Christ.
